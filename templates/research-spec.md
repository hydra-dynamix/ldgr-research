# Research program spec

## Objective

One sentence: what would change our behavior if this research succeeds?

## Guiding questions

The questions this program exists to answer. Each should become a branch,
open question, or classified research option.

## Constraints and boundaries

What is out of scope, what resources are bounded, what must not be touched.

## Evidence standard

What counts as validation for this program's claims: commands, datasets,
artifacts, review, or reproducible manual checks. Important claims should have
explicit falsification criteria and evidence-linked accepted facts, not repeated
observation alone.

## Known assumptions

Starting beliefs. Record durable assumptions as facts or axioms so the ledger can
force their validation, contesting, or retirement.

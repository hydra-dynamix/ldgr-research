# Research milestones

One milestone per research program phase. Milestones are lightweight planning
artifacts; the durable state lives in `ldgr-research` programs, branches,
options, experiments, facts, decisions, and reports.

| Phase | Milestone | Achieved when |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| Framing | `<slug>-framed` | Objective, guiding questions, and initial classified options recorded |
| Evidence | `<slug>-evidenced` | Core hypotheses have completed experiments and evidence-linked accepted facts |
| Synthesis | `<slug>-synthesized` | Findings report artifact recorded; limitations and open questions explicit |

Record milestone progress as observations, artifacts, decisions, or report
sections. If a phase implies more research, queue one concrete next hypothesis
or option rather than a broad placeholder.
